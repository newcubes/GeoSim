🔬 Experiment: Material Physics Parameters

Purpose: Test and understand material parameters: ID, density, viscosity, and angle of repose.

Key Insight: Material IDs are sequential (0,1,2,3...) and don't represent density. Angle of repose is controlled by behavior pattern, not a separate parameter.

Note: This is an experimental version. The original is preserved at sand-simulator-hybrid.html.

Material Properties Reference

Material ID: Sequential assignment (0-11 for core, 12+ for extended). NOT directly related to density.

Density: Physical property stored in material definitions. Used for density-based swapping.

Viscosity: Controls liquid flow rate. Formula: moveChance = 100 / (viscosity^0.25)

Angle of Repose: Maximum angle a pile can form. Controlled by behavior pattern (POWDER vs STURDYPOWDER), not a separate parameter. For powders, this is like viscosity for liquids.
